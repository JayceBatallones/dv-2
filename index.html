Electric Vehicle Trends

Electric Vehicles (EV) have seen an increase in adoption over the past couple of years. They have been found to be more cost-effective compared to their gas powered alternatives and have also been improving each year. An increase in both range, capacity, and safety have made these appealing to new vehicle buyers.

Additionally, the increase in the cost of living, oil prices and government subsidies have also aided in EV adoption. Buyer sentiment has slowly changed around EV’s and more people than ever are looking into buying them. The following will showcase the trends of EV’s.

Types of Electric Vehicles

EV’s come in a few different forms. There are two key types we will focus on Battery Electric Vehicle’s (BEV) and Plug-in Hybrid Vehicles (PHEV). BEV’s having purely a battery, whilst PHEVs have a both a battery, electric motor, gasoline tank and a combustion engine. BEV’s are more efficient but PHEV are a good alternative for those who might have range anxiety.

Electric Vehicle Manufacturers

There are many automakers that have been making EV’s. Tesla in particular has been the most successful EV auto manufacturer producing over 51,000+ vehicles for the state of Washington. This is a significant number compared to its competitors. Chevrolet is the second highest with a total amount of 5217 vehicles. This is nearly a 10x difference between the first and second best manufacturers.
